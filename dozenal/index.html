dozenal Suite

The dozenal suite is a set of programs designed to assist with working in the dozenal (also called duodecimal or base twelve) system. It includes number converters, an RPN calculator, a graphical calculator, a metric system converter, a pretty-printer, and a date-and-time program.

The programs included are:

  • doz: converts decimal numbers into dozenal. Works for integers, floating point, and exponential ("scientific") notation.
  • dec: converts dozenal numbers into decimal. Works for integers, floating point, and exponential ("scientific") notation.
  • dozdc: a Reverse Polish Notation (RPN)) calculator. Works entirely in dozenal. Understands a large variety of arithmetical, trigonometric, and other functions.
  • tgmconv: a metric system converter; converts units from TGM, SI metric, customary, and imperial measures.
  • dozpret: a pretty-printer for dozenal numbers. Includes many options for radix points, separators, digit groupings, and transdecimal digits. Also includes preset options for LaTeX output.
  • dozstring: a simple byte converter; accepts a string of characters, and converts any numbers into dozenal.
