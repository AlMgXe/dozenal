dozcal: A Tutorial

Dozcal is an extremely powerful program, and learning to use it effectively will do a world of good, as well as enable you to use the power of your computer to help you keep your calendar in dozenal. We'll address things in steps: first, we'll address how to let dozcal know what you want to be kept in your calendar; then, how to make dozcal display what you want in the way you want it; and finally, how to automate dozcal's behavior so that it will work that way without you having to tell it to every time.
